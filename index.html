Brief description


This website contains the final document for the study “Migration and housing: a study of rental costs in 2017” within the course “SOC01073-1 Methodological profundization elective - Open Social Sciences: Tools for Reproducibility, Colaboration and Comunication in Social Investigation 2020, First Semester”
